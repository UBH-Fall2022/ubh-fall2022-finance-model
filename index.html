Welcome to FisionTech!

Introduction

FisionTech is a Financial Calculator that follows the 50/30/20 Rule. The User inputs their Weekly Wages, Yearly Salary as well as the total amount of their savings and checking accounts combined. The Goal of FisionTech is to make saving more approachable as well as easy to understand for everyone. Once the User inputs the necessary data, FisionTech will show the user a graph of how their savings should be split up.

What is the 50/30/20 Rule????

According to Investopedia, The rule is to split your after-tax income into three categories of spending: 50% on needs, 30% on wants, and 20% on savings.

  • 50% of your income should be allocated for Paying bills and on essential items needed for survival such as grocery shopping or rent payments.
  • 30% of your income can be allocated for non-essential spending such as paying for vacations, buying takeout,etc. This 30% is highly optional.
  • 20% of your spending should be allocated to put back into your savings and investment accounts. Examples include placing money into the Stock Market or future bill payments. The 20% ensures that in the future, should something bad happen, you will still have a safety net to rely on.
