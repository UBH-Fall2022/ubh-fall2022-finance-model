What is FisionTech?

  • Fision Tech is a platform where you calculate the amount of money you should be saving based on your income. Saving and managing your funds will help set aside your future and upcoming financial goals you wish to achieve. Furthermore, this helps create a budget and control your expenses will increase your savings overtime.

Why is this important to us?

  • Applying the 50/30/20 rule to your income will help you manage your money after taxes, set aside some money to use when in an emergency and save for retirement. Many people like to spend their entire paycheck without thinking about their future financial goals. We wanted to take part in this encourage people to spend their money wisely and not raise debt rates.
